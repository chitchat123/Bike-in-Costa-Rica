Lodging

Lodging can go from basic to very nice!!
Of course, in every option, they will be nice and clean, but also of course, the better the lodging, the nicer it will be!!
In our Basic option, Lodging can be in basic hotels (usually called “cabinas”, a little better than a hostel, because rooms sometimes can be for just 2 people.).
In our Comfort and Full Support options, rooms are always double (may triple in some occasions), and with nicer facilities.
If you have any doubt, again, please, don’t hesitate to ask!!
